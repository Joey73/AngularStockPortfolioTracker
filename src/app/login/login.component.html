Abbreviation Explanation
Total-Income Market-Value minus Effective-Cost
Effective-Cost Total-Cost minus Dividends
EPS Earnings per Share
DPS Dividend per Share
DPR Dividend Payout ratio = DPS divided by EPS (should be < 70%)
ROE Return on Equity (should be > 10%)
P/E Price to Earnings ratio (Trailing 12 Month or Forward) = Current-Stock-Price divided by the EPS (should be < 20)
